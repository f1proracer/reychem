About us

A UK compliance partner built for Indian exporters

Most REACH consultancies were built for large European companies with in-house regulatory teams. We exist for everyone else — specifically, Indian chemical manufacturers and formulators who need expert help without the corporate price tag or the jargon.

Who we are

UK-registered. India-focused.

Reychem is a REACH compliance consultancy registered in England & Wales. We help Indian chemical manufacturers, formulators, and exporters navigate the UK REACH and EU REACH regulatory frameworks — so they can sell into the UK and European markets legally and confidently.

We're not a large corporate firm. We're a focused, accessible practice that combines genuine regulatory expertise with a straightforward client relationship. You talk to us directly. We manage the technical specialists on your behalf. No layers of account management, no inflated fees.

🇬🇧

Registered in the UK

Your REACH obligations exist under UK law. We're based where your compliance obligations sit.

🇮🇳

Built for Indian business

We understand Indian export operations, timelines, and how to make the process work across time zones and regulatory systems.

🔬

Real technical specialists

We coordinate a network of experienced REACH toxicologists and regulatory consultants — the same calibre used by multinationals.

Why Reychem exists

The compliance gap no one was filling

When India-to-UK chemical trade accelerated after Brexit, a clear gap emerged: Indian exporters needed a UK-based REACH partner who understood their situation. Large consultancies were expensive and built for European clients. We set out to close that gap.

💬

Plain English, not regulatory jargon

We don't assume you know what SVHC, DUIN, ATRm, or CLP mean. We explain what each requirement means for your specific products and what you need to do about it — in plain language.

📋

Transparent, fixed-scope quotes

Every quote is based on your actual substances, tonnage, and situation. We tell you exactly what needs to happen and what it costs, before you commit to anything.

🤝

A single point of contact

You deal with Reychem. We coordinate the technical specialists, manage the dossiers, and keep you informed — you don't juggle multiple contractors or translation layers.

⚡

Starting point: always free

Every client starts with a free REACH check. We look up your substances, identify what you'll need, and give you a plain-English summary — at no charge, with no obligation.

How we work

Coordinator model: your one contact, our specialist network

We act as your project coordinator and single point of contact. The specialist technical work is carried out by our network of qualified REACH consultants.

🔗

What this means for you

REACH dossiers, IUCLID submissions, toxicological assessments, and chemical safety reports require deep specialist knowledge. By coordinating a network of experienced consultants rather than maintaining a large in-house team, we can give you expert-level technical quality at a more accessible price point. You get one relationship — with us — and we handle everything else.

📁

IUCLID & dossier work

Handled by REACH-qualified specialists with direct ECHA experience. We manage and review every submission before it goes in.

🧪

Toxicological assessments

Where required, we commission professional toxicological and chemical safety assessments from qualified consultants in our network.

📄

SDS & CLP labelling

Safety data sheets and CLP-compliant labels prepared to UK and EU standards — reviewed and signed off by authorised personnel.

🎓

Training & consulting

Bespoke training for your regulatory, procurement, or export teams — covering REACH fundamentals, UK REACH differences, and CLP obligations.
